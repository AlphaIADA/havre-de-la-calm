Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2025

At OTA Apartments (Havre De La Calme), we are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the data you provide when using our website and booking our services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name, phone number, and email address (via booking forms or contact pages)
  • Check-in/check-out details and guest preferences
  • Browser and device data (via cookies and analytics tools)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To confirm and manage your apartment bookings
  • To send booking confirmations and relevant communication
  • To improve our services and website experience

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except where required by law or for processing secure transactions (e.g., payment gateways or analytics providers).

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use Google Analytics and Facebook Pixel to understand site usage and improve ads. You may choose to disable cookies through your browser settings.

5. Security

Your data is protected through secure forms and encrypted channels. We regularly monitor for vulnerabilities to keep your information safe.

6. Your Rights

You can request access to or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at hdlc.bookings@gmail.com.
